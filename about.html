Part Of Nature

A sustainability initiative
Take pride in being a part of nature

Part of nature is an initiative to explore ancient lifestyle that incorporated nature friendly and sustainable practices, to blend it with modern requirements; and also to create awareness on sustainability.

It aims to reduce burden on Mother Nature and to appreciate the human element and diversity in art and craftsmanship.

More about Part of Nature

Revolves around the concept of refuse, reduce, reuse and recycle

Aims to work with organic and renewable materials, and encourage sustainability in environment, society and economy

By exploring old practices, Part of Nature aims to bring down environmental footprint in production, use and disposal

Part of nature also aims at management of waste produce by creating a cradle to cradle product cycle and following the product throughout its life cycle
